Locked Coins

Locked coins is a value that displays the amount of the coin supply not used incrypto trading and which are instead stored in the project community investment portfolio. Premine can also be connected to this value - this sum is also reflected in the wallet balance of the Giant developers. Overall, the Locked Coins value can show the general level of trust of the project users and its reliability.
